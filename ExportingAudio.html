Exporting Audio Basic Info

Exporting audio is the last step when creating a recording. It is useful for being able to hear your work in other media applications or players.

Exporting Audio Steps

  • Step 1, click on the file tab at the top of your screen and then select export
  • Step 2, once export is selected you will choose your file type, which in this case we will be doing MP3
  • Step 3, after selecting the MP3 file type there will be encoding options below where we will change the bit rate to constant which gives the best quality
  • Step 4, we will then select the joint stereo channel mode
  • Step 5, at this point you can rename your file and choose the file destination on your pc
  • Step 6, you will then be asked to fill out the metadata tags which are optional, once you are done press ok and then you are done exporting
