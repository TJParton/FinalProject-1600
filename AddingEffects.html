Adding Effects Basic Info

Adding effects to your recording is what makes it sound professional and put together, without effects for the most part your raw audio will not sound very good. Using effects is a good way to touch up your recording and give it that professional sound

Adding Effects Steps

Now that you have your recording its time to add in some effects, for this tutorial we will be using the built in effects that Audacity has to offer.

  • Step 1, highlight the portion of audio that you wish to add an effect onto
  • Step 2, navigate to the top of the screen and select effects
  • Step 3, click your selected effect for this example we will use reverb
  • Step 4, after clicking reverb you can change the values and then preview your new audio
  • Step 5, if you are satisfied with your previewed audio you can click ok and the audio effect will be applied
