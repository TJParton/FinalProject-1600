Sample rate basic info

Sample Rate is the number of samples of audio carried per second. The sample rate determines the maximum audio frequency that can be reproduced. 44,100 Hz is the default setting in Audacity and it is recommended that you keep it at this frequency.

Setting sample rate

  • Step 1, ctrl + p or navigate to preferences
  • Step 2, click on quality and from there you can set sample rate
  • Step 3, below sample rate you can change sample format if you wish
