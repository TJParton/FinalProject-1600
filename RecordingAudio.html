Recording audio basic info

To record audio in Audacity you will need a microphone or audio mixer.

Recording Audio Steps

  • Step 1, start by pressing the click to start monitoring button, this will let you test your microphone and allow you to make sure it is not peaking by going up to the 0 recording level as you want to remain below that but not too far below
  • Step 2, when you are ready click the red record button at the top
  • Step 3, speak into the microphone
  • Step 4, once you are satisifed with what you have said, you will want to click the square stop button at the top
  • Step 5, if you wish to listen to your recording you can play it back by pressing the green play button at the top
